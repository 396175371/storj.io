Pre-sale FAQs

What are the details for the Storj Token Pre-sale?
Participation in the pre-sale is based on purchase size, with larger purchase amounts receiving priority. Purchases of the same size are prioritized by date received. Due to high demand, it is likely that the pre-sale will reach its cap and orders smaller than $50k are unlikely to be eligible for participation in the pre-sale.

During the main token sale, tokens will be priced at $0.50 per token. Discounts are available for the following order types:
  • Between $50k-100k: 10% discount, which is $0.45 per token, with a lock-up of 3 months
  • Over $100k: 20% discount, which is $0.40 per token, with a lock-up of 6 months

There is still an opportunity to participate in the main token sale, starting May 19th, 2017 at 11:00 EDT (3:00 PM UTC). The token sale will be held at https://storj.io/tokensale
When does the Storj Token Pre-sale end?
The Storj Token Pre-sale will end on May 19th, 2017 at 11:00 AM EDT (3:00 PM UTC), when the main token sale begins.
How will I know if I get to participate in the Storj Token Pre-sale?
We will contact you about participation in the Storj Pre-sale. Please remember that purchase orders are first prioritized by size (larger orders receive priority), then on a first-come, first-serve basis. Interest in the token sale has increased, and orders under $50k may not be eligible for participation.
How do I pay for tokens in the Pre-sale?
If you are participating in the Token Pre-sale, we will send you detailed instructions via email once you confirm your willingness to participate.
When will the Pre-sale requests be processed? When will I see my tokens?
All tokens purchased during the pre-sale will be distributed via time-locked smart contracts simultaneously with the other tokens. Your provided address will have sole control of the smart contract. This means that you will need to wait for the main token sale to start to see your tokens.
