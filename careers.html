Software Engineer

Job Description

Are you an amazing Node.js engineer? Are you passionate about decentralization, free and open source software, and the blockchain? Do you want to change the way people own data? Have you ever geeked out over novel p2p pub/sub systems? If so, we’d would love to meet you!

We’re looking for full-time engineers for our core technology and flagship applications. You will be part of the core team of an early-stage startup looking to release a product ASAP. You should be passionate, dedicated, and live to create, like we do. You will own large portions of our product, and shouldn’t be afraid to push it in the direction you think is best.

This is a full time position in our office.


Skills & Requirements

Our ideal candidate has:

  • A passion for decentralization, open source software, and the blockchain
  • Deep knowledge of and experience with Node.js
  • Impeccably clean code beautiful enough to make grown men weep
  • An obsessive, near-creepy, love for unit tests with maximum coverage
  • A practical understanding of public-key cryptography and secure design
  • All the icing on the cake of a modern engineer: Github, design patterns, etc.

Bonus points for:

  • Experience working with Bitcoin or another cryptocurrency on the protocol level
  • Deep knowledge of C/C++, Java, Python, or Go
  • Experience building high-featured RESTful web services
  • Experience with React, or similar front-end frameworks

Super extra bonus points for:

  • Contributions to open-source projects in decentralization or cryptocurrency
  • Advanced degree (M.S., Ph.D.) in a relevant field
  • Experience or background in distributed algorithms, machine learning, artificial intelligence, complexity science
  • Developing your own awesome blockchain-based technology

Data Scientist

Job Description

We have some big questions about our storage network. How can we guarantee 99.999% availability (or better!) to our customers? How much does past behavior predict future behavior? Who should we contract to store data? We’re looking for someone to help us get some big answers.

This is an open-ended job built around a complex problem: given information about nodes on a peer-to-peer network, including storage capacity, uptime, relative latency, and bandwidth, how do you distribute data storage across nodes to meet or exceed availability and accessibility goals? If this seems interesting to you, well, we can pay you to solve it!

This is a full time position in our office.


Qualifications

  • An advanced degree in a relevant field
  • Competent in a scripting language (Python preferred)
  • Can design, test, build, and explain complex statistical
  • Impeccable code quality, and ability to work in a cooperative development environment
  • Passionate about decentralization, free and open source software

Responsibilities:

  • Design and implement statistical methods to inform peer selection in our network
  • Understand customer needs and look for interesting ways to meet them
  • Explain the processes involved to the team, and work closely with them to bring them to production
  • Provide and maintain short and long-term roadmap

Technical Content + Community Manager

Intro

At Storj Labs, our mission is to provide the simplest experience to manage object storage infrastructure online. We are looking for an experienced community leader to build an active and engaged membership that will embody the company’s philosophy and mission.

As the Community team’s first hire, you will be the first point of contact with developers and bleeding-edge tech lovers across the globe. Your role will include producing and curating a collection of educational content for the community, as well as engaging and supporting community members on an ongoing basis, so they have a truly amazing experience with our products.


Who you are

  • A talented writer who produces and curates content in volume, with meticulous attention to detail and a knack for what people want to consume
  • A champion of online forums and social media, who is often found exploring and responding to threads on Reddit, HackerNews, Twitter, etc.
  • A lover of innovative technology, who can’t wait to share the latest awesomeness with the world
  • A generalist who wears many different hats and understand the nuances of different platforms and subcommunities

What the job entails

  • Produce, edit and curate content that the community loves
    • Develop unique, shareable content across a variety of lengths and formats
    • Galvanize community members and internal team members with specialized knowledge to produce how-to guides, opinion pieces and technical content that aligns with their expertise (both paid and unpaid)
    • Keep an active pulse on conversations worth sharing, and transforming them into content that can be distributed to the greater community
    • Manage an editorial calendar and a team of contributors to produce content on a regular basis
    • Collaborate with an SEO expert to maximize content discoverability
    • Work with marketing team on company blog posts, social media posts, email copy and website copy, as well as content distribution
    • Report on high-level content metrics via analytics platform and qualitative measures
  • Manage, engage and support the community on a day-to-day basis
    • Develop a voice and personality for the community in your capacity as the on-the-ground, accessible face of the company
    • Encourage and moderate discussions and debate, while mitigating destructive behaviors and trolling
    • Create a strong governance for community interactions, while maintaining a decentralized, self-organized feel
    • Keep the community updated of the latest developments at Storj, and champion their feedback back to the team
    • Proactively identify and forge relationships with influencers and thought leaders, and connect like-minded members where appropriate
    • Show appreciation and goodwill through discounts, swag and other rewards
    • Maintain FAQ, contribute to help desk and support issues resolution
    • Track and report on key high-level metrics (upvotes, re-tweets, support tickets)

Your minimum qualifications

  • 2-4+ years working in a copywriting, content marketing, community management or technical support role, preferably in a developer focused and/or technologically focused company
  • Excellent written and verbal English communication skills
  • Working knowledge of Amazon S3, Microsoft Azure and Google Cloud Object Storage offerings

Growth and Automation Lead
