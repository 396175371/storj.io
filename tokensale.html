Why use a token?

We recently announced a migration of the Storj token, STORJ (formerly SJCX) to the Ethereum ERC20 standard from the Counterparty protocol on the Bitcoin blockchain. This move helps us reduce transaction fees for the Storj community while also increasing functionality and usability.

There are many benefits to using STORJ:

Granularity

Bitcoin isn’t divisible or flexible enough to support the granularity needed for micropayments.

Stability

A separate token like STORJ provides a layer of protection from the volatility of larger markets.

Scalability

Using STORJ helps us identify and fix problems at scale, paving the way for a larger, more stable network.

Incentivization

We pay for our community's hard work performing tasks like tier-one tech support and development with STORJ.

Micropayments

A network funded hub would allow for quick transactions, as well as keep transaction fees low for the users.

Partnerships

Storj Labs is dedicated to building long-lasting partnerships and supporting the broader [Ethereum] ecosystem.

Ecosystem

Users can earn STORJ for sharing disk space, then use it as renters themselves, creating a positive feedback loop.

Future Growth

We plan to use the token in ways raw Bitcoin or Ether can’t. For example, the Storj-specific signaling described in SIP0002.

Storj is designed to be flexible and modular.

We believe that using a token is the best payment system for the network.

How Storj Works

Storj is a peer-to-peer network comprised of “farmers,” users who rent out their spare hard drive space and bandwidth, and "renters" who purchase space and bandwidth.

Storj is up to 10x faster and up to 50% less expensive than traditional datacenter-based cloud storage solutions. Storj is a platform, a token, and a suite of tools for developers looking to build better tools and applications.

Storj Token Sale FAQs

How can I participate in the Storj Token Sale?
In order to participate in the token sale, you will need:

  • A receiving Ethereum address - This Ethereum address must be from an ERC20 compatible wallet (such as Parity, Mist, MetaMask, and MyEtherWallet). Your STORJ tokens will be delivered to this address.

    We cannot fill your order unless you have an Ethereum account with an ERC20 compatible wallet.

  • A BTC refund address if funding with BTC

  • An email address - For sale-related notifications. We won't ever disclose your email to third parties.

Receiving and refund addresses cannot be exchange addresses, unless you own the private key.
Where will the Storj Token Sale be held?
We will be running the token sale through a web application, located here at storj.io/tokensale.
How can I pay for tokens?
We accept Bitcoin (BTC) or Ether (ETH). Fiat will not be accepted. We suggest you start preparing your funds as soon as possible.
Where should I send funds?
Once the Token Sale opens, you will be able to access the sale address using a special web application. We will never, ever send you an address requesting money directly (unless you are the part of the pre-sale). Please be mindful of scammers and check out our FAQ.
What Ethereum wallet can I use?
You can use any ERC20 compatible wallet, such as Parity, Mist, MetaMask, and MyEtherWallet.
Who can I talk to?
You can join the Storj Token Sale discussion on our Telegram channel.

We have several moderators there to provide support and answer questions:

  • Marina Khaustova @khaoust from The Argon Group
  • Nic Minichiello @cryptobubble from the Storj Labs Community
  • Helene Unland @heunland from the Storj Labs Community

They are best equipped to support you. Please be mindful of who you communicate with during the Storj Token Sale. If you're not sure of any communication, please reach out to one of them.
When is the Storj Token Sale?
The Storj Token Sale will begin on May 19th, 2017 at 11:00 AM EDT (3:00 PM UTC). The sale will close when the cap of $30MM is reached, or on June 19th, 2017 at 11:00 AM EDT (3:00 PM UTC), whichever is sooner.
What is the pricing for the Storj Token Sale?
STORJ tokens will be priced at $0.50 each. Purchase orders of $50k+ may be eligible for a discount during the pre-sale. Participation in the pre-sale is limited.
When will I get my STORJ tokens?
All tokens purchased during the Storj Token Sale will be distributed within four (4) weeks of the end of the sale.
