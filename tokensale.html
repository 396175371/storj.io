How does Storj work?

Storj is a peer-to-peer network comprised of “farmers,” users who rent out their spare hard drive space and bandwidth.

Storj is up to 10x faster and 50% less expensive than traditional data center-based cloud storage solutions. Storj is a platform, a cryptocurrency, and a suite of applications for developers looking to build better tools and applications. By using blockchain technologies, Storj users don’t have to assume the risk of censorship or hacking as with data centers.

Why use a token?

We recently announced a migration of Storj cryptocurrency, STORJ (formerly SJCX) to the Ethereum ERC20 standard from the Counterparty protocol on the Bitcoin blockchain. We made this move to help us reduce network transaction fees for the Storj community while also increasing functionality and usability.

But why do we use STORJ instead of an established coin like Bitcoin or Ether in the cryptocurrency ecosystem? Here’s a quick rundown:

Granularity

Bitcoin isn’t divisible enough to support the granularity needed for micropayments.

Scalability

Using STORJ helps us identify and fix problems at scale, paving the way for a larger, more stable network.

Flexibility

Using STORJ allows us to migrate to the accepted standard, without being locked into a particular technology.

Stability

A separate token like STORJ provides a layer of protection from the volatility of larger markets.

Incentivization

STORJ compensates our community members for their hard work in performing tasks like tier-one tech support, marketing, documentation, and development.

Micropayments

A network funded hub would allow transactions to be quick and only require low transaction fees for the users.

Partnerships

As Storj grows, we are interested in supporting the broader [Ethereum] ecosystem and building long-lasting partnerships.

Ecosystem

Our token provides a positive feedback loop within the Storj ecosystem. Farmers can earn STORJ tokens for sharing their disk space, and then use it for renting data storage services themselves.

Future Growth

We plan to use the token in ways raw Bitcoin or Ether can’t be used. For example, the Storj-specific signaling described in SIP0002

Storj is designed to be flexible and modular.

We believe that using a token is the best payment system for the foreseeable future.
